Lili & Michal are getting married

We are very happy to announce that we will say "I do" on August 17, 2024, at Rybniční zámeček in Lednice (Rybniční zámeček 437, 691 44 Lednice na Moravě). We can't wait to share this special moment with you.

The ceremony will begin at 12:30 PM, but we kindly ask everyone to arrive by 12:00 PM.

What is happening throughout the day?

Approximate schedule:

  • 11:00 – 12:00 Arrival of guests
  • 12:30 – 13:00 Wedding ceremony
  • 13:15 – 13:45 Photoshoot
  • 14:00 – 15:00 Lunch
  • 15:30 – 16:00 Congratulations and toast with the wedding party, group photos, sweet refreshments
  • 18:00 – 20:00 Buffet
  • 20:00 – Wedding cake
  • 20:30 – First dance by the bride and groom, followed by dancing for all guests. We invite each guest to take us for a dance personally
  • 22:00 – 5:00 The party continues in the cellar for those who stay

All our guests are invited to participate in the entire program.

What about wedding gifts?

Please, no gifts. We would greatly appreciate a financial contribution towards the banquet instead.

Can I choose my meal and drinks?

Please fill out a short questionnaire so we can order the right meal and drinks for you.

A few final points

  • This is an outdoor wedding (don't worry about rain—we've arranged for good weather and a wedding tent). We recommend wearing shoes suitable for walking on grass or loose surfaces.
  • Please keep your phones away during the ceremony and let our professional photographer capture the moments.
  • Feel free to express your emotions; our photographer loves natural, candid moments.
  • Vegetarian and vegan dishes will be available.
  • For those without reserved accommodations, you are welcome to dance the night away or pitch a tent on the meadow.

We look forward to celebrating with you!

About me

Hi, my name is Michal Palas and I work as a data scientist in Vienna (Austria). I hold a Master's degree in Economics and have a strong foundation in Mathematics, Statistics, and mathematical modelling. One day I decided to interrupt my PhD in Economics and dive into the world of data science. I have never regretted this decision. I enjoy facing new challenges almost every day, whether it is tuning a machine learning model, exploring hidden patterns in the data, or evaluation of various business strategies. If I am not sitting at my computer, you may find me outside doing my favorite sports - climbing or beach volleyball.

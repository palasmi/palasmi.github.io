Lili & Michal are getting married

We are going to say our YES on August 17, 2024 at Rybniční zámeček in Lednice (Rybniční zámeček 437, 691 44 Lednice na Moravě) and we are already looking forward to your company at this important moment.

The ceremony starts at 12:30, but we ask everyone to arrive no later than 12:00.

What is going to happen all day?

Approximate schedule:

  • 11:00 – 12:00 Arrival of guests
  • 12:30 – 13:00 Wedding ceremony
  • 13:15 – 13:45 Photoshoot
  • 14:00 – 15:00 Lunch
  • 15:30 – 16:00 Congratulations and toast with the wedding party, photography of smaller groups, sweet refreshments
  • 18:00 – 20:00 Buffet
  • 20:00 – Wedding cake
  • 20:30 – Dance of the bride and groom. Brave guests join in after the first dance and invite the bride and groom to dance together
  • 22:00 – 5:00 The strongest stay and move to the cellar for the party

All our guests are invited to the entire program.

What about wedding gifts?

Please, don't purchase any gifts. We will be very grateful for a financial contribution to the banquet.

A few final points

  • This is an outdoor wedding (don't worry about the rain, we have booked good weather and a wedding tent). We recommend our guests to choose shoes with which they will be able to walk even on loose surfaces, for example on grass.
  • We ask our guests to leave their phones in their pockets during the ceremony and leave the photoshooting to our professional photographer.
  • Don't be shy to express your emotions, even in front of our photographer, who, like us, prefers natural, unplanned moments.
  • Vegetarian and vegan dishes will also be available.
  • For guests who do not have reserved accommodation, we offer the possibility to spend the night dancing, or to pitch a tent on the meadow.

We are looking forward to you.
